Monday, December 5

Welcome Dinner
5:00 PM - 7:00 PM

After getting checked-in to the event and the hotel, join us for a social dinner in the hotel's private dining room. Any important announcements regarding the week's activities will be made during this gathering. This is also a good opportunity to pick-up any supplemental material you may need for this week's activities!

Tuesday, December 6

MAFCA Board Meeting
9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Members of the MAFCA Board will meet to discuss upcoming events and vote on decisions pertaining to the club's operation. This event is required for members of the MAFCA Board of Directors, although club members are welcome to attend between 9:00 and 10:00 to ask questions or present any suggestions.

Colorado Railroad Museum Tour
10:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Join us for a short drive to the Colorado Railroad Museum, home to several railroading artifacts, rolling stock, and locomotives rich with Colorado's unique railroading history. Tour the roundhouse, learn about the museum's collection of standard- and narrow-gauge equipment, and even enjoy the model railroad in the basement of the depot.

Hubley Races
2:00 PM - 4:30 PM

MAFCA's version of the Pinewood Derby races - Hubley's are vintage small-scale, die-cast models of the Model A Ford, which MAFCA members can assemble and race. While the Hubley Tournament itself is held during MAFCA National Conventions, this race is a great opportunity to test your Hubley against others from around the country.

Wednesday, December 7

Technical Seminar: Different Types of Overdrives
9:00 AM - 10:00 AM

A popular modification performed to make the Model A Ford into a better touring car is the addition of an overdrive transmission. This seminar will cover the different types of overdrives available for the Model A Ford, coompare how they work, and describe the installation and operation of each variant.

Fashion Seminar: Formal Attire
9:30 AM - 10:30 AM

This seminar will discuss the different types of formal attiire typically worn during the Model A Ford era. Following an examination of the style itself, members of the MAFCA Era Fashion Commitee will discuss best practices for finding and maintaining original formal clothing, as well as what to look for when purchasing reproduction- and era-image formal attire.

Gymkhana: Car Games (Weather Dependent)
1:00 PM - 4:30 PM

Drivers will be challenged with a number of fun games involving driving the Model A Ford, including "Fishing on the Move," parallel-parking, and Bumper Pendulum Bullseye. Please note that this event is dependent on weather conditions. A back-up seminar about marketing the Model A Ford to younger age groups will be held in the case of inclement weather or unsafe conditions.

Thursday, December 8

Buffalo Bill's Grave / Lariat Loop Tour
9:00 AM - 1:00 PM

The Model A Ford Club of Colorado will lead a tour up to the top of Lookout Mountain via the Lariat Loop Scenic Byway. Upon reaching the summit, we will visit the grave of Buffalo Bill Cody, known for his traveling show in the days of the Wild West. A lunch stop at a local restaurant will be included as part of this tour.

MAFCA National Awards Banquet Dinner
6:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Celebrate the past year's travels and accomplishments with the MAFCA Board of Directors! We will welcome newly-elected members of the MAFCA Board, present awards for Chapter Service, Newsletter and Website awards, and highlight upcoming national events scheduled for 2023 and 2024!
